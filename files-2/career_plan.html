Summary

The IT job market in 2024 heavily favors candidates with a blend of programming fundamentals (Python, SQL), cloud literacy (AWS, Docker), and domain-specific expertise (ML, data visualization). By following the structured improvement plan from the Skill Gap Analysis and pursuing targeted certifications, each team member can significantly improve their competitiveness within 90 days.
