Navigating the Job Market

A data-driven analysis of hiring trends, skill demand, and career outlooks from the Lightcast job postings dataset.

About this project

We are Team 1 of AD688 (Spring 2026) five analysts exploring how the U.S. job market is evolving in the age of AI, remote work, and shifting industry demand. Drawing on thousands of job postings from the Lightcast dataset, we investigate where the opportunities are, which skills matter most, and how geography and industry shape career outcomes today.
